Cranberry Sauce

Description

This cranberry sauce recipe uses fresh cranberries, sugar, and orange juice to make a Thanksgiving classic.

Believe it or not, this homemade cranberry sauce comes together quickly and easily with just three easy-to-find ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Cranberries
  • Sugar
  • Orange Juice

Steps

  1. Heat orange juice in a saucepan.
  2. Stir in sugar and cook until it dissolves.
  3. Add cranberries and continue cooking, stirring often, until they start to pop.
  4. Remove the sauce from heat and transfer it to a bowl - the cranberry sauce will continue to thicken as it cools.
